Jauz Unleashes Two Crazy Collaborations

Jauz and Netsky teased us with a small clip of their collab “Higher” last month, and today it finally arrived in full. It dropped on Zayne Lowe’s Apple Music show, and it definitely doesn’t disappoint. With a bouncy synth and vocals that are right in Netsky’s wheelhouse, this song with pull insane reactions on the dance floor. At the same time his unreleased collab with Eptic popped up on Soundcloud and boy does it bang.

Cookie Monsta Resurrects The “Wobble” On New EP

In a time where some STILL say “dubstep is dead” (LOL), the one and only Cookie Monsta is bringing back the old school wobble on his newest EP. “Wobble” is a four-track that actually serves as a history lesson reminiscent of his hit “Let’s Get Wobbly” which dropped over two years ago.
